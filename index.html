SCAM 01 / 05
💼
FAKE
JOB
OFFERS

Fraudsters post lucrative WFH jobs on Telegram, WhatsApp & fake job portals. They ask for a "registration fee" or "training deposit" upfront — then vanish with your money.

🚩 RED FLAGS
Any job that asks for money before you start. No verifiable company address. Salary "too good to be true".
Never pay to get a job
Verify on LinkedIn
Check MCA portal

SCAM 02 / 05
📱
OTP &
SIM
SWAP

Scammers impersonate telecom agents or bank reps and trick you into sharing OTPs or getting your SIM swapped — giving them full access to your bank accounts and UPI apps.

🚩 RED FLAGS
Unexpected call from "KYC team." Sudden loss of mobile network. Urgency around "SIM expiry."
Never share OTP with anyone
Activate SIM lock
Use 2FA via app

SCAM 03 / 05
🏦
INVEST
MENT
FRAUD

Fake trading apps, crypto "gurus," and Telegram groups promise guaranteed 30–200% returns. Often structured as Ponzi or pump-and-dump schemes targeting first-time investors.

🚩 RED FLAGS
"Guaranteed returns." No SEBI registration. Pressure to recruit others to earn more.
Verify SEBI registration
No guaranteed ROI exists
Report on Cyber Crime portal
@genalpha.in
SCAM 04 / 05
👮
DIGITAL
ARREST
SCAM

Scammers pose as CBI, narcotics, or customs officers on video call. They claim a parcel with drugs was intercepted in your name and demand money to "close the case." Even PM Modi warned about this.

🚩 RED FLAGS
Real police NEVER arrest over video calls. Demands for immediate payment or wire transfer. Threats of "immediate arrest."
Hang up immediately
Call 1930 helpline
No such law exists

SCAM 05 / 05
💬
WHATS
APP
SCAMS

From fake lottery wins to "wrong number" romance scams and malware APK links — WhatsApp is India's #1 scam delivery channel. Clicking unknown links can silently install spyware on your phone.

🚩 RED FLAGS
Unknown international numbers. "You've won a prize." Links asking for bank/Aadhaar details. Asking you to install an APK file.
Block & report immediately
Never install APKs from chat
Turn off auto-download
